01 — About

Who I am

I studied at Zoho School of Learning, a program that puts real work before theory. Rather than sitting through lectures, I was thrown into actual projects — building websites, running campaigns, reading data, and picking apart products.

I enjoy experimenting with marketing ideas, building websites from scratch, analysing data to find patterns, and understanding how products are designed and grown. This portfolio is the record of everything I made and learned along the way.
